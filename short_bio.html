Jade Pederson

I am a recent graduate from the Environmental Planning, Bachelor's of Science Program at the University of Iowa. In my academics, I tended to focus on issues of sustainability and sustainable development. Professionally, I have interned for the Planning Department for the City of Iowa City. In my studies and in my professional life I have gained proficiency in GIS software, Microsoft Office, and RCommander. I have a passion for evaluating modern development and consumption patterns and identifying areas for improvement in environmental, social, and economic aspects. I plan to continue work in urban and regional planning for the foreseeable future.
